AB  - We read with great interest recent scientific letters addressing abdominalaspects of COVID-19. Martin-Falquina IC et al. reported the thrombotic nature of acute pancreatitis (AP) in a patient with COVID-19 and likewise, our patient withAP was previously anticoagulated. The association in the medical literaturebetween viral infections (such as those caused by the human immunodeficiencyviruses, hepatitis C and influenza) and venous thromboembolism (VTE) has againbecome evident in the scientific community, both in 2017 during the epidemic ofchicungunya and Zika, and more recently in the current COVID-19 pandemic.
